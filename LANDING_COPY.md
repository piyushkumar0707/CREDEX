## FAQ

**Do I need to connect billing data?**  
No. The MVP uses manual inputs so founders can get value without granting account access.

**Is this a sales form?**  
No. The audit runs before email capture. Email is only requested after the report is visible.

**How accurate are the savings estimates?**  
They are directional but traceable. Every rule is based on official pricing data and simple plan-fit logic.

**What happens if my stack is already efficient?**  
The report says so. It does not manufacture fake savings.

**When does Credex appear?**  
Credex is surfaced prominently when the audit finds more than $500/month in potential savings.
